# TikTok Ads Tools

Adspirer provides tools for TikTok Ads campaign creation and asset management.

## Available Tools

| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `create_tiktok_campaign` | Create a TikTok image ad campaign |
| `create_tiktok_video_campaign` | Create a TikTok video ad campaign |
| `discover_tiktok_assets` | Find reusable assets in your TikTok account |
| `validate_and_prepare_tiktok_assets` | Validate assets meet TikTok's ad specifications |

## Supported Campaign Types

### In-Feed Image Ads
Static image ads that appear in the TikTok For You feed. Best for brand awareness and click-through campaigns.

### In-Feed Video Ads
Video ads that blend naturally into the TikTok feed. Supports various aspect ratios and durations per TikTok's specifications.

## Asset Requirements

TikTok has specific asset requirements. Use `validate_and_prepare_tiktok_assets` before campaign creation to ensure your assets are compliant:

- **Video:** 9:16, 1:1, or 16:9 aspect ratio; 5-60 seconds
- **Image:** 1200x628px (landscape) or 640x640px (square)
- **Text:** Ad description up to 100 characters
- **Brand name:** Up to 20 characters
