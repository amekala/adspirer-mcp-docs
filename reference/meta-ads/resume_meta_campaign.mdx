<Badge color="yellow">Write</Badge>

User wants to resume a paused Meta campaign.

IMPORTANT: Resuming a campaign restarts ad delivery. Budget will start being spent again.

Returns:
- Confirmation that campaign is active
- Campaign details
- Ads Manager URL

When to use this tool:
- "Resume my Meta campaign"
- "Turn on my Facebook ads"
- "Reactivate campaign 123456"
- "Start my paused Instagram campaign"

Parameters:
- campaign_id: The Meta Campaign ID to resume (required)

Execution time: 2-5 seconds
Effect: Campaign status changes to ACTIVE immediately

## Parameters

<ParamField path="campaign_id" type="string" required>
  The Meta Campaign ID to resume (required)
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="ad_account_id" type="string">
  Meta Ad Account ID. Required for multi-account users. Get from list_connected_accounts.
</ParamField>

## Example prompt

Ask your AI assistant (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, etc.) something like:

```
Resume Meta Campaign for my account.
```

Your AI client will call the `resume_meta_campaign` tool behind the scenes.
