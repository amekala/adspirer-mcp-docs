<Badge color="green">Read-only</Badge>

Get current audience signals for a Performance Max campaign.

Returns audience signal resource names and associated audience resource references.

**Use when:**
- User wants to see what audience signals are on their PMax campaign
- Before adding new signals
- To get resource_name for removal

**Parameters:**
- campaign_id: The PMax campaign ID

**Execution time:** 1-2 seconds

## Parameters

<ParamField path="campaign_id" type="string" required>
  The Google Ads campaign ID (numeric). Example: '21854471508'
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="customer_id" type="string">
  Google Ads customer ID. Required for multi-account users. Get from list_connected_accounts.
</ParamField>

## Example prompt

Ask your AI assistant (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, etc.) something like:

```
Get PMax Audience Signals for my account.
```

Your AI client will call the `get_pmax_audience_signals` tool behind the scenes.
