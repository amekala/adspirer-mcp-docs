# Connect Claude

Claude has native MCP support, making it the easiest way to get started with Adspirer.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Claude Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the MCP Server">
    - **Name:** Adspirer
    - **URL:** `https://mcp.adspirer.com/mcp`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Complete the OAuth 2.1 sign-in flow. This securely links your Adspirer account without sharing passwords.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link Ad Accounts">
    On first use, ask Claude to connect your ad platforms:

    ```
    Connect my Google Ads account
    ```

    Follow the prompts to link Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, or TikTok Ads.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
**Verify:** Ask Claude "Show me my active campaigns" to confirm everything is connected.
</Check>

## Example Prompts

Try these to get started:

- "What's my Google Ads ROAS this month?"
- "Research keywords for [your product] and show me CPCs"
- "Create a Performance Max campaign for [your goal]"
- "Analyze my LinkedIn ad performance and suggest improvements"

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Claude doesn't show MCP tools">
    Make sure you're using Claude Desktop, Web, or iOS with MCP support enabled. The connector should appear under Settings → Connectors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="OAuth sign-in fails">
    Try clearing your browser cookies for `adspirer.com` and reconnecting. If the issue persists, contact [support](mailto:abhi@adspirer.com).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
